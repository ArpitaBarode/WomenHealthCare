OUR PROJECT

Poor women, especially in rural areas, struggle to get a clean piece of cloth to manage their period every month. There is a lack of awareness about menstrual health and hygiene. Living in a culture of shame and silence, they end up using poor substitutes like rags and ash, or worse they use NOTHING.Help us make a difference in the lives of women and girls by donating sanitary pads today. A small act of kindness can have a big impact. Your donation of 35/packet can change lives.Help us create a healthier and happier future for all. Period poverty is a real issue, but together, we can provide dignity and relief.
